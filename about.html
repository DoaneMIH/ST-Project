OUR STORY

Everyone merits great wellbeing and at “Angelfel Pharmacy”, your wellbeing and prosperity are our need.

For more than 10 years, we have thought about the network with an extreme vision of making a more advantageous town, one part at any given moment.

The first branch of pharmacy is Angefel Pharmacy located at Purok 3, Real St., San Joaquin, Iloilo and the second branch is located at Prince Hypermarket at Purok 5, San Joaquin, Iloilo. After a year, Gloria Estonilo who is the founder decided to have another branch on Brgy. Inawangan for the benefit of the patients in the community hospital. Angelfel Pharmacy is actually their third branch of pharmacy located at Purok 5, Brgy. Inawangan, San Joaquin, Iloilo right across the Mother and Child Community Hospital. Gloria Estonilo and her daughter Christine Estonilo are both registered pharmacists.

The establishment offers different kinds of pharmaceutical needs and services like: Allergy medications, Cough and cold medications, syringes, Diet, nutrition, and meal supplements, Family planning, First aid, Home medical, Personal hygiene, Skin care, Stomach and acid reflux medications, multivitamins and other products.

Angelfel Pharmacy is already open at around 6 o’clock in the morning until 7:30 in the evening. The pharmacy consists of only 1 pharmacists and 2 pharmacy assistants.
